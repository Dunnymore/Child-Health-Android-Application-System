Heat and Light Sensitive Vaccines

Below is the information regarding the sensitivity of various vaccines to heat, light, and freezing temperatures:

Vaccine Heat Sensitivity Freezing Sensitivity Storage Temperature
OPV Sensitive to heat Not damaged by freezing +2°C to +8°C
Measles Sensitive to heat and light Not damaged by freezing +2°C to +8°C
BCG Relatively heat stable, but sensitive to light Not damaged by freezing +2°C to +8°C
Hepatitis B Relatively heat stable Freezes at -0.5°C (Should not be frozen) +2°C to +8°C
DPT Relatively heat stable Freezes at -3°C (Should not be frozen) +2°C to +8°C
TT and DT Relatively heat stable Freezes at -3°C (Should not be frozen) +2°C to +8°C

NOTE: At the PHC level, all vaccines are kept in the ILR for a period of one month at a temperature of +2°C to +8°C.
